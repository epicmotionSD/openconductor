## The Challenge

### The Product
x3o.ai is a white-label salon booking platform that deploys **autonomous AI agents** to recover lost revenue for beauty businesses:

- **Ghost Client Revival**: Reactivates inactive customers via personalized outreach
- **Conversation Recovery**: Auto-responds to abandoned DM conversations
- **Instant Slot Filling**: Fills last-minute cancellations automatically

### The Problem
These AI agents:
- Handle customer conversations without human oversight
- Make financial decisions (bookings = revenue)
- Operate across multiple businesses (multi-tenant risk)
- Process personal data (GDPR + AI Act exposure)

**Regulatory Reality:**
> "EU AI Act, effective August 2026, classifies AI systems that interact with customers and influence economic decisions as **high-risk**. Violations: up to €35M or 7% global revenue."

**The Gap:**
Traditional SaaS infrastructure provided NO way to:
- ❌ Verify agent identity (who is this agent?)
- ❌ Establish ownership chain (who's responsible?)
- ❌ Audit agent actions (what did it do?)
- ❌ Obtain insurance (no identity = no policy)
