## üé® Visual Branding Guidelines

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Electric Blue (#0066FF) - Progress, technology
- **Secondary**: Bright Green (#00FF88) - Success, checkmarks
- **Accent**: Purple Gradient (#8B5CF6 ‚Üí #EC4899) - Premium, modern
- **Dark**: Near Black (#0A0A0A) - Terminal backgrounds

### Typography
- **Headings**: Inter Bold, 72pt
- **Body**: Inter Regular, 48pt
- **Code**: JetBrains Mono, 36pt
- **Captions**: Inter Medium, 40pt (with black background)

### Animation Style
- **Fast & Snappy**: Match the "10 seconds" theme
- **Smooth Transitions**: 0.3s ease-in-out
- **Satisfying Checkmarks**: Green ‚úì with bounce
- **Progress Bars**: Animated fill, not stepped
- **Terminal Text**: Typewriter effect (150ms per char)

### Key Visual Motifs
1. **The Timer**: Always visible during installs, bold, counts up
2. **The Clipboard**: Animated copy icon when system prompt copies
3. **The Checkmark**: Green, bouncy, satisfying

## üí° Pro Tips for Maximum Impact

### Recording Tips
1. **Use a real workflow** - Show actual use cases, not contrived examples
2. **Keep terminal clean** - Dark theme, large font, no clutter
3. **Highlight the magic moments** - Clipboard copy, instant install, system prompt paste
4. **Show genuine reactions** - If recording face, let excitement show
5. **Multiple takes** - Record each scene 3x, pick best

### Editing Tips
1. **Pace is everything** - Match the "10 seconds" energy
2. **Cut ruthlessly** - No dead air, no slow moments
3. **Music matters** - Build-up, payoff, energy
4. **Captions required** - 85% of social video watched on mute
5. **Hook in 3 seconds** - First 3 seconds determine if people watch

### Distribution Tips
1. **Time your posts** - 9am PT (tech Twitter prime time)
2. **Engage immediately** - Reply to first 10 comments within 5 minutes
3. **Cross-promote** - Share Twitter on LinkedIn, YouTube on Twitter, etc.
4. **Leverage community** - Ask early users to share
